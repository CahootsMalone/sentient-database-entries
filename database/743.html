T'Geniv III Ore Mine

T'Geniv III has just come online after its second rebuilding. This was caused by anti-government demolition teams, who leveled T'Geniv II five years ago. Terrorists were making wild accusations, claming the government was not mining for ore, but rather mounting a large-scale architectural dig for ancient weapons technology rumored to exist on Guelni. The Magistrates of Pohl expect an arrest shortly.
