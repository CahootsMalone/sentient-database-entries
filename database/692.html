Wisstin

The largest city within the Confederation of Democratic Provinces L1/L2 L3/L4, Wisstin continues to export cash crops for use as food and textiles. These crops are grown near-organically within the mile-high gigaplex that is Wisstin. It is noteworthy in that it contains the University of Prill at Vardin on the twenty-first level, the leading center of learning within the Confederation of Democratic Provinces L1/L2 L3/L4.
