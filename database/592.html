Livan'chu

Self-styled "freedom-fighters", the religious zealots of the Livan'chu defy the will of the Collective by asserting that the planet Malfic is theirs by divine right. Claiming that they are the chosen of the Dah MeYar, the Livan'chu have staged a twenty year guerrilla war against the Hegemony and their Bloodbound Warriors, possibly aided in secrecy by the Sorority of Navigators. Efforts to crush their activities have been successful, as the Zercesians have eliminated a full two thirds of their population.
