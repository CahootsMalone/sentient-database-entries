Ellis

Ellis is often referred to as the "big city at the end of the universe". Although Quor has a much higher population, the urban center of Ellis is considered the reason for traveling to the Pohl System. Ellis has a reputation across the Seven Systems as the vice capitol of the New Hegemony, featuring cyber-brothels, Smart-Game hunting, and all manner of pharmaceuticals. Many a citizen has been heard to shout "Off to Ellis!" upon receiving his year-end bonus from his employer.
