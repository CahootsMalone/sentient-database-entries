Navigational Lane

The points at which Navigators are able to warp space, allowing for travel between stars. Because these points only exist within a certain distance of a star, and each star is attuned to only one other star, travel to remote regions of the galaxy is prohibited. As a result, Navigational Lanes are hotly contested areas of space. When a Navigator opens a Navigational Lane, what they call "touching the Ka-Mi-Nah", all other Navigators within the vicinity become aware of her presence.
