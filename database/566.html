Bellimost Empire

The second of the Vell Dynasties, the Bellimost Empire was founded three hundred years after the death of Crynost. During that time, imperial systems were fractured into world states and controlled by warlords. A little known admiral named Bellimost was able to ally with enough former naval officers to control the Navigator's trade lanes. Through the use of diplomacy and shrewd business tactics, Bellimost was able to rise as the second of the Emperors.
