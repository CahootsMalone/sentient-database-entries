Jatilla Temple

Attempts to determine the age of this structure place its construction before the migration of refugees to the Frontier. Although some claim that the building was discovered and adopted by the Navigators, others believe that the Sorority knew there would one day be a need for it , and so began construction hundreds of years before the Exodus. Whatever the case, the Jatilla Temple is the religious center of the New Hegemony and the home to the Sisters of the Dah MeYar.
