Frontier

The edge of the known universe. After fleeing the Death's Head Missile L1/L2 L3/L4, the remnants of the Torindine Empire fled through the only navigable star left not destroyed. This star led to the Frontier of Seven Systems. No other systems are reachable from these seven stars, and so, the Frontier is effectively the end of the universe. These Seven Systems form the entirety of the New Hegemony.
