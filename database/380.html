BRIEF BACKGROUND
Mason Garrilac joined the Confederated Corps of Engineers just after quitting trade school. He served on several low-level crews before the Confederation was co-opted by the Hegemony. It was then that an internal aptitude test confirmed his abilities. He was immediately sent to the Foristan Project where he distinguished himself as an Asset to the Hegemony, thus upgrading his rank and unofficial status. Twenty-three years and five demanding missions later, Garrilac has been singled out as an individual who can travel great distances and complete complicated assignments under adverse or dangerous conditions.
