Pohl

A poor system five warp points from Nephlon. Near the edge of the navigable galaxy, Pohl contains a gas giant with nine moons: Gava, Quor, Letti, Ringoa, Falarn, Kurg, Guelni, Helarga, and Jor. While some of these moons support life, the population is near exclusively made up of tertiary citizens, who are put to use as manual laborers.
