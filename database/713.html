Battle Over Krisbrun

Senator Corrin Doralus-Tain L1/L2 L3/L4 and his three eldest sons were killed when their assault-corvette was shot down over the one of Marlith's vast deserts during the first invasion. The Senator, realizing death was imminent, managed to steer the corvette into the Livan'chu L1/L2 L3/L4 stronghold, and thus win the battle. The senator's youngest son was placed aboard a supply ship loaded with the Livan'chu L1/L2 L3/L4 treasury. Unfortunately, the ship never arrived at the Nephlon System, thus ending the Doralus-Tain line.
