Pellin Doralus-Tain

The youngest son of Senator Corrin Doralus-Tain L1/L2 L3/L4, and lost during the Battle of Krisbrun. Pellin Doralus-Tain is distinguished as the only noble heir ever born on Marlith soil. This occurred while his father was stationed there as Regional Governor. Pellin's mother died during a 32 hour labour, mid-wived by an acolyte of the Jatilla Temple during the festival of the Lorimer Alignment. The boy was originally thought to be afflicted by a birth defect which later was diagnosed as a benign birthmark on the back of his neck.
