Banagoon

Banagoon is a medium red star three warp points from the New Hegemony capitol of Nephlon. It is the location of the Banagoon Nebula, a vast cloud of cosmic gas that has been known to interrupt navigation. It is also one of the Imperial Protectorates, and as such, has seen its share of radical political demonstrations and terrorist activities. The Banagoon System contains two planets: Banag and Theris.
