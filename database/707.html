Marlith

Currently, control of this desert planet is hotly contested by the Bloodbound acting on behalf of the Grand Senate, and the indigenous population, the Livan'chu L1/L2 L3/L4. Of special note is the presence of the Sorority of Navigators Jatilla Temple, located within the city of Tracine. The only other city of note is Jerrole, as it contains the Boristol Indigenous Population Control Center L1/L2 L3/L4.
