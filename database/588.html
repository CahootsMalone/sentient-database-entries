Bloodbound Warriors

The Zercesians, who bound themselves in perpetual servitude to the Imperial Senate, vowing to die in their service. This bond is an unshakable blood oath of fealty that restrains the Zercesian from uttering a word against his master or raising a hand against him. They are likewise bound by a code of honor called Chulkah. The Bloodbound are truly the most awesome warriors the galaxy has ever seen.
