Mason Garrilac

FILE NUMBER
PCF11076

PSYCHOLOGICAL PROFILE
Garrilac's Devil-May-Care/Joker attitude is a facade aimed at diffusing potentially hostile situations. He has developed this mask over the course of his long career in several hot spots in the Protectorates. Underneath this disrespectful exterior is a complex mind, serious and loyal to his men.

KNOWN GROUP AFFILIATIONS
Confederated Corps of Engineers-disbanded

SUSPECTED GROUP AFFILIATIONS
Confederated Corps of Engineers-- although subsumed by the New Hegemony super-structure, it is believed that this group still maintains contact for political/labour reasons. Internal Security Level: 3 Confidential on Need to Know

FAMILY
None - Garrilac left Therig just before the 7th Marines devastated the planet for non-compliance with the Senate's Protectorate Codex of Actions.

CRIMINAL RECORD
Disorderly Conduct--served seven days in Barraga Correctional Facility
Drunken and Disorderly Conduct--served one month in Yetchee Correctional Facility

POLITICAL OFFICER'S COMMENTS
With almost no advanced education, it is a wonder that Garrilac has progressed as far as he has. However, it is quite clear that the man has both brains and experience, as well as a natural affinity for the workings of complex machinery. He is fiercely protective of his charges, however, and during his stay on Debirick, Garrilac went so far as to threaten a superior officer with physical violence for cutting back rations to Garrilac's men. This, coupled with Garrilac's affiliation with the shadowy Confederated Corps of Engineers makes him a liability. Status TERMINATE
