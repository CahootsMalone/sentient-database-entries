Senator Corrin Doralus-Tain

Died along with his sons Camar, Gonn, and Bengin as heroes of the Collective at the Battle Over Krisbrun. Doralus-Tain supposedly attempted to smuggle his three-year-old son Pellin Doralus-Tain out of the Veetoh System aboard a supply ship, however, a ship with similar markings was reported shot down over the desert while attempting to flee system. No survivors were found.
