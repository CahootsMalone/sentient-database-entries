Barraga

Barraga, a small yellow star, has the distinction of being the only sun in history to allow navigational lanes to two other systems: Veetoh and Nephlon. As such, this system contains the highest density of population, and enjoys its status as the commercial center to the galaxy. Unfortunately it is also the center of the Confederation of Democratic Provinces L1/L2 L3/L4, and dedicated to free trade without Hegemony tariff restrictions. It contains three planets: Attag, Churl, and Yetchee.
