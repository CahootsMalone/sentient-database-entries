Telepathic Bond

Called by the Navigators, the Doh-Vet-Nahr. According to researchers, the Navigators' psychic connection while using their power is transmitted via tachyon pulses emitted in the alpha band of the subatomic continuum. This band is thought to be readable with a Tachyon Tracker.
Members of the Yysblan ThinkTank have been working to reproduce this phenomenon so as to synthesize the Sorority's navigation powers. In this way, the Hegemony's dependence upon the strong-willed Navigators might be ended.
