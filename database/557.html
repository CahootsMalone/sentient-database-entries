Torindine Empire

The Torindine Empire was the last of the great Vell Dynasties. Forged from the ruins of the Bellimost Empire, the Torindine Empire was marked by the near fanatical devotion of Emperor Torindal to exploration and conquest. During the Torindine Reign, the Imperial Navy rose as the greatest power ever to control the void between the stars. It also saw the greatest loss of life imaginable when the Imperial Navy discovered a navigation lane leading to the Sle'Stacheyar L1/L2 L3/L4.
