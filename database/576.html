New Hegemony

Founded soon after the Exodus, the New Hegemony is truly a marvel of vision and purpose. Dutifully maintained by the Imperial Senate, the Hegemony prospers and flourishes as it spreads across the Seven Systems rekindling the glory of the Torindine Empire. Upon exploring the Seven Systems, the refugees discovered several enclaves of indigenous population. Upon discovering the vast resources they had to offer, the New Hegemony offered them limited citizenship in the form of an annexed Imperial Protectorate. Today, these races work and live with the Torindine Progeny and enjoy the benefits that serving the Collective can bring.
